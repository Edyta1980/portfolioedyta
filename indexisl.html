O mnie

I was a managing editor for one of the weekly papers of "Dziennik Bałtycki"Before that, I worked as a journalist for many magazines, for example "Głos Pomorza", "Echo Podlasia", "Gazeta Extra" or "Tina". I started with writing articles for the Internet portal called O2.pl. I also worked for a local TV station for a few months. However, the job for "Dziennik Bałtycki" gave me the most of experience, knowledge... and emotions. A journalist position in a local paper is a real school of life. It does not only include writing texts for the paper, but also taking photos, making and editing videos... or calling local companies and encouraging them to take part in sms contests.

Thanks to this experience, I have learned a lot.I can take photos, make and edit videos or short films. As I was responsible for a website for many years, and I have attended many courses on this topic, I know how to write texts ros search engines or social media.

Now it's time for new challenges. I have always wanted to work in IT. For many years I regretted that I had chosen cultural studies as my second filed of stury. Therefore, I decided to test myself a bit in IT. branży. I udało się. Skończyłam studia podyplomowe na kierunku Junior Web Developer. I still need to learn a lot but I'm ready for that!

Career objective

I finished high school in Ciechanowice. Then I studied Polish philology at Akademia Podlaska, today known as The Siedlce University of Natural Sciences and Humanities. I was among the first graduates specilised in text editing. We were taught how to work for magazines and science publishing houses.

However, instead of editing someone else's texts, I mostly wrote my own.I started working in an editorial office during my studies. Later, I cooperated with the Internet portal O2.pl. This was very interesting. I learned most from working for "Głos Pomorza" and "Dziennik Bałtycki". I learned there how to write articles, or even talk to... unhappy readers.

Working for a local newspaper was a real school of life As a managing editor, I was responsible for my coworkers' work, preparing the issue for print, checking all the spelling, and mistakes. I had to know how to talk to an angry and unhappy reader who came to us to complain about town services. It was not only writing texts for the paper, but also creating content for the website, updating it with news, photos and videos. To umiejętności negocjacji, kiedy It wasn't always so easy!
